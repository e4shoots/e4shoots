About Elia Bernaldes

e4shoots started as just me picking up a camera and trying to capture moments the way I actually see them. Over time, it turned into something I can’t really put down — a way for me to slow things down and notice the small details in everyday life.

I’m a high school student doing all of this on my own, learning as I go with every shoot and edit. It’s just me, figuring things out, staying curious, and trying to keep getting better while turning real moments into something that lasts.
